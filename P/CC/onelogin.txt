User management in cloud (onelogin - proton mail)

For signup/registering: https://www.onelogin.com

Click on free trial enter your details

Now login to your onelogin account

For adding users: users-> Add users 
Enter details of users

For adding groups: users->click on any user ->authentication->add groups-> enter group details

For adding application: click on any users->applications->"+"->search application->click on any application-> click on save user button
